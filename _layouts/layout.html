My New Year's resolution for 2014 is to make more pixel art! I've been dabbling for the past while and I really want to get better at doing something I love.

Enter Fifty-Two Weeks. My aim is to create a new and unique piece of pixel art each week for 52 weeks. I'm limiting each piece to 64px x 64px and will only be using the NES colour palette. Each peice will be posted by the end of the week (usually Saturday).
